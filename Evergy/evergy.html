Evergy Project Overview

Welcome to the Evergy Project Overview page! This project aims to explore and enhance the career opportunities and website experience for Evergy. Our efforts have been divided into two key areas:

1. Survey and Data Analysis: We conducted a comprehensive survey to understand the career preferences and perceptions of potential line workers in Kansas City. The results provide valuable insights into the factors influencing career choices and highlight opportunities to improve awareness and perception of line worker careers.

2. Website Redesign Mockups: Our talented students have reimagined the Evergy website, creating mockups that make the site more engaging and user-friendly. Their innovative designs reflect a fresh perspective and a keen understanding of user needs, ensuring a better overall experience.
