Our distinctive program, offered exclusively to schools in the PREP-KC area, is dedicated to cultivating the next generation of tech innovators. Through a pioneering partnership with Oracle, students from four Kansas City metro districts—KCPS, Center, Grandview, and Hickman Mills—immerse themselves in technology for two hours daily at an Oracle campus.

Our curriculum is hands-on and dynamic, enabling students to gain practical experience in programming and app development, while exploring the vast array of careers in technology. This showcase is a testament to the dedication and creativity of our high school participants.

Our students have made impressive strides in the tech world, contributing to open-source projects, securing internships at Oracle, and developing a range of video games using platforms like Unity, Roblox, and Python. This year, we are particularly excited to highlight a collaboration with Pathways to Design students, leading to the creation of two visual novel games using Ren'Py. Students from both pathways have joined forces, with design students producing compelling assets for these games.

We invite you to explore and play the demo versions created by our talented students. Witness firsthand the potential and innovation that Pathways to Tech brings to technology education!
